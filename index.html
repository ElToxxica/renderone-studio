The Studio

Quiet rooms. Cinematic light. Furniture that earns its place.

Render One Studio is an interior design and visualization practice working at the intersection of architecture, photography, and atmosphere. We design residential interiors and translate them into images that feel less like renders and more like memories.

Every project begins with a question: what should this room make you feel the moment you walk in? Everything else — material, palette, scale, light — follows from that single answer.

— How We Work

From brief to final frame.

i

Listen

We start with conversation, not catalogues. Mood, memory, and how you want a room to feel.

ii

Direct

Concept boards, palette, and reference photography. The film of the room before it's filmed.

iii

Render

Modeling, lighting, and post — treating each frame the way a cinematographer treats a scene.

iv

Deliver

A full set of stills, walkthroughs, and a refined material list — ready for press, build, or both.
